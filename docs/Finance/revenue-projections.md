# Revenue Projections

## Month 1
- **$900** from **3 basic audits**

## Month 2
- **$2,700** from **5 basic audits + 1 audit + fix**

## Month 3
- **$5,400** from **7 basic audits + 2 audit + fix engagements + 1 retainer**

## Quarter 1 total
- **$9,000**

## Assumptions
- The service sells first as a low-ticket audit and then converts a portion of clients into audit + fix or retainer work.
- Delivery capacity is assumed to be available through AI-agent execution, so the model does not include payroll or contractor expense.
- Retainer pricing is assumed to start at the low end of the published range for the forecast period.
- Revenue is recognized when the audit or service milestone is delivered.

## Risks
- **Pipeline risk:** actual client volume may be lower than forecast.
- **Conversion risk:** not every audit will convert to an upsell or retainer.
- **Scope risk:** remediation work may require more effort than expected if the repository is complex.
- **Pricing risk:** buyers may negotiate discounts or request custom scoping.
- **Timing risk:** retainers may start later than planned, shifting revenue into future months.

## Financial takeaway
The forecast is intentionally conservative on operating cost and aggressive on gross margin. With a zero-cost delivery model, the main constraint is client acquisition, not service capacity.
