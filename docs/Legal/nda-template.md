# Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)

This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (“Agreement”) is between **JAGM IT Company** (“JAGM”) and **Client**.

It applies when the parties share information for a client engagement, including a repository audit or related advisory work.

## 1. Confidential Information

“Confidential Information” means any non-public information shared by either party, including:

- source code, scripts, workflows, and configuration files
- audit findings, notes, recommendations, and draft reports
- business information, pricing, processes, plans, customer details, and technical documents
- any other information that should reasonably be understood as confidential

Confidential Information may be shared in any form, including written, electronic, oral, or visual form.

## 2. Confidentiality obligations

Each party agrees to:

- use the other party’s Confidential Information only for the client engagement
- keep that information private and protect it with reasonable care
- share it only with people who need to know it for the engagement and who are bound by similar confidentiality obligations
- not copy, publish, sell, or disclose it to any third party without prior written consent, unless required by law

## 3. Exclusions

Information is not Confidential Information if the receiving party can show that it:

- was already known without a duty of confidentiality
- becomes public through no fault of the receiving party
- was lawfully received from a third party without a confidentiality duty
- was independently developed without using the other party’s Confidential Information

## 4. Return or deletion

When the engagement ends, or sooner if requested, each party will stop using the other party’s Confidential Information and will return or delete it within a reasonable time, unless keeping it is required by law.

## 5. Term

This Agreement starts when both parties accept it and remains in effect for **2 years**.

The duty to protect Confidential Information shared during the term continues for the full 2-year period, even if the engagement ends earlier.

## 6. No license

Nothing in this Agreement gives either party any ownership rights or license rights in the other party’s Confidential Information, except the limited right to use it for the engagement.

## 7. Governing law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of **Ukraine**.

## 8. Simple agreement

The parties agree that this document is meant to be practical and easy to use for client work. If a stronger custom NDA is needed, the parties may replace this template with a signed version tailored to the engagement.
