# Service Agreement: GitHub Repository Audit

This Service Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into between **JAGM IT Company** (“Service Provider”) and the customer purchasing the service (“Client”).

## 1. Service
The Service Provider will perform an **automated audit of one GitHub repository** designated by the Client. The audit will focus on the following areas:

- secrets exposure
- CI coverage
- branch protection
- security policy presence and basic completeness
- code quality indicators

## 2. Deliverable
The Service Provider will deliver a **Markdown report** containing findings, observations, and practical recommendations based on the audit.

## 3. Timeline
The final report will be delivered **within 48 hours from payment receipt**, provided the Client supplies the required repository access or repository URL promptly.

## 4. Fee and Payment Terms
The service fee is **USD $300**.

If the Service Provider offers the service to a first-time client at a promotional discount, the fee may be reduced by **20% to USD $240**.

Payment is due as follows:

- **50% upfront** before work begins
- **50% on delivery** of the report

## 5. Client Responsibilities
The Client will provide access to the repository or any needed information in a timely manner. The Client confirms it has the right to share the repository for audit purposes.

## 6. Confidentiality
Both parties agree to keep the repository contents, audit findings, and any non-public information confidential. Neither party will disclose such information to third parties except as required to perform the service or as required by law.

## 7. Limitation of Liability
To the maximum extent permitted by law, the Service Provider’s total liability under this Agreement will not exceed the **service fee actually paid** by the Client for the audit.

## 8. No Guarantee
The audit is an automated review and does not guarantee that all issues, vulnerabilities, or defects will be identified.

## 9. Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of **Ukraine**.

## 10. Acceptance
By paying the invoice for this service, the Client agrees to the terms of this Agreement.
