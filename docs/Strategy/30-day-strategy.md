# JAGM IT Company — 30-Day Strategy

## Executive Goal
In the next 30 days, JAGM IT Company will prove a repeatable, zero-budget sales engine for our GitHub Repo Audit service and win our first paying client within 7 days. The immediate revenue target is **$300 from the first client**. This first win is the launch point for higher-value audit+fix engagements and monthly retainers.

## Core Offer
Our product is a **GitHub Repo Audit** designed to help software teams reduce risk, improve code quality, and ship faster.

**Offer tiers**
- **Basic Audit — $300**: fast repo review with prioritized findings and quick wins.
- **Audit + Fix — $1K-$3K**: audit plus implementation support for high-impact issues.
- **Retainer — $1K-$5K/month**: ongoing repo health, security, delivery, and quality support.

The $300 entry offer is our conversion wedge. It is simple, easy to buy, and fast to deliver.

## Market Focus
We will focus on two segments:
1. **Ukrainian software companies** that need practical engineering support, stronger delivery discipline, and affordable technical audits.
2. **International startups** that need rapid quality, security, and CI/CD improvements without hiring full-time staff.

Our message: **“We find the issues that slow shipping, create risk, and reduce code quality — then we show you exactly how to fix them.”**

## 30-Day Plan

### Days 1–7: Close the first client
- Build a tight lead list of 50–100 qualified targets.
- Send personalized outbound messages to founders, CTOs, engineering managers, and product leads.
- Offer a short discovery call and a low-friction $300 basic audit.
- Use urgency and clarity: one audit, one repo, one deliverable, fast turnaround.
- Goal by Day 7: **one paid engagement closed**.

### Days 8–14: Deliver excellence and create proof
- Deliver the first audit quickly and professionally.
- Turn the audit into a case study, testimonial, and before/after story.
- Identify upsell opportunities for fixes or retainer work.
- Document the delivery process so the team can repeat it.

### Days 15–30: Scale outreach and convert bigger deals
- Expand outreach to more Ukrainian companies and international startups.
- Improve the audit template, report structure, and sales script based on real feedback.
- Push qualified prospects into the **audit + fix** and **retainer** tiers.
- Target 3–5 active sales conversations at all times.

## Team Model: 18 AI Agents
We will operate like a lean AI-powered delivery company. The 18 agents will be assigned to three functions:
- **Sales and prospecting agents**: list building, outreach drafts, follow-ups, objection handling.
- **Delivery agents**: repo analysis, findings prioritization, report drafting, remediation support.
- **Operations and quality agents**: templates, QA, consistency, project tracking, and knowledge capture.

This structure lets us move fast with zero budget while maintaining quality and responsiveness.

## Operating Principles
- **Zero budget discipline**: no paid ads, no paid tools unless absolutely unavoidable.
- **Manual-first sales**: personalized outreach beats automation at the start.
- **Speed wins**: close, deliver, and learn faster than competitors.
- **Proof over polish**: one strong case study is more valuable than broad branding.
- **Upsell from trust**: every audit is an entry point to higher-value work.

## Success Metrics
By Day 30, we should have:
- 1+ paying client from the $300 offer
- 3–5 qualified ongoing opportunities
- 1 published case study or testimonial
- A repeatable audit delivery process
- A proven list of high-fit prospects for the next cycle

## CEO Priority
My role over the next 30 days is to keep the team focused on revenue, move quickly on sales conversations, and ensure every delivery creates proof that can be reused. Our mission is not just to complete an audit — it is to build the first repeatable revenue system for JAGM IT Company.
